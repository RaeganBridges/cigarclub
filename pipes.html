New to pipes? Start here

Choosing a first pipe should be fun, not intimidating. Here is how we help you get started.

Find your fit

We will help you pick a shape and size that feels comfortable in hand and suits how and where you like to smoke.

Match the tobacco

From bright Virginias to smoky English blends, we will pair your new pipe with a tobacco that matches your taste.

Learn the ritual

Packing, lighting, tamping — we will walk you through the basics so your first bowls smoke cool and even.

Care and keeping

Simple cleaning habits keep a good pipe smoking well for decades. We will show you what actually matters.

Come see the pipe wall in person

Photos never do grain justice. Stop by the lounge and find the pipe that fits your hand.
