Frequently asked questions

Do you offer a military, police, or EMS discount?

Yes. We offer a 10% discount for Military, Police, and EMS — just mention it when you check out in the shop.

I am new to cigars — can you help me choose?

Absolutely. Tell us what flavors you enjoy and how much time you have, and we will point you toward a blend that fits — no pressure, no jargon.

How do I find out about tastings and events?

Our Events page stays synced with The Cigar Club Google Calendar. New tastings and lounge nights appear there as soon as they are published.

Where are you located?

We are at 2148 Gallatin Pike N in Madison, TN. Tap the address on our Location page for directions.
